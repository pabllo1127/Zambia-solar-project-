ZESCO Joint Venture — SPV Structure

Based on the JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited precedent (Chirundu 100 MW, USD 90M, December 2025)

The structure you want already exists and is active. JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited is an SPV with shareholding split between Jigsaw Investments Limited (private developer) and ZESCO Limited. It is building the 100 MW Chirundu Solar Plant right now. You want to replicate this model.
Chirundu plant — deal parameters
Project: 100 MW solar PV plant · 285 hectares · Chirundu District
SPV: JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited
Shareholders: Jigsaw Investments Limited + ZESCO Limited
Total cost: USD 90 million · Plant life: 25 years
Grid: 14 km, 330 kV line to Kafue Gorge Lower switching station
Groundbreaking: December 2025
ZESCO MD statement (December 2025)
"The project is being developed through a joint venture between Jigsaw Investments and ZESCO. We want to partner with the private sector — the private sector brings its knowledge, skills and efficiency in delivering projects, and we share risk with the national utility."
ZESCO investment mandate
ZESCO has planned to invest USD 6.4 billion from 2023–2031. Financing through "debt and equity complemented by Special Purpose Vehicles and Public Private Partnership model." 81% of installed capacity is owned by ZESCO and its subsidiaries. They need private technical partners to deliver their solar pipeline.
Three parties. One SPV. Permanent equity. Both you and ZESCO keep your stakes for the full 25-year operational life.

What you bring
Engineering credentials (CEng / EIZ / EngRB) · Full operational management for 25 years as CEO / Technical Director · Project development and feasibility study · On-site presence (not an absentee investor) · HV protection relay design and ZESCO interconnection expertise
What ZESCO brings
Capital facilitation (DFI debt via AfDB, IFC, EIB relationships) · Land access and site preparation · Grid connection at existing 33/66/330 kV substation · Government permitting pathway and ZEMA approvals · Institutional credibility that de-risks the project for lenders
Key negotiating point
Open with a 70/30 split (you 70%, ZESCO 30%). ZESCO may push for majority — hold firm on the basis that you are providing full operational management for 25 years, which is the single most valuable contribution to a long-life asset. Minimum acceptable: 51% (you retain control). Engage Corpus Legal Practitioners or Musa Dudhia & Co (Lusaka) for SHA drafting.
