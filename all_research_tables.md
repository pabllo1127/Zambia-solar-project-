## Table 2 — Solar PV Technology Comparison (2025)

| Technology | Module Efficiency | Cost USD/W | Key Advantage |
|------------|------------------|------------|---------------|
| Standard monocrystalline PERC | 20–22% | $0.85–$1.10 | Proven, lowest cost |
| Bifacial monocrystalline | 21–23% | $0.90–$1.20 | +10–30% yield from rear |
| TOPCon (JinkoSolar Tiger Neo, LONGi Hi-MO X10) | 24–24.8% | $1.00–$1.30 | Highest commercial efficiency |
| HJT (SunPower Maxeon 7, AIKO Neostar) | 24.1–26% | $1.20–$1.50 | Best low-light performance |
| Thin-film (CdTe, CIGS) | 10–13% | $0.50–$0.80 | Not recommended for Zambia |

Sources: Solar Tech Online (2025); Cedar Creek Energy (2024); Hometown Station (2025)
