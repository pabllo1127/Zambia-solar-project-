# 08 — ZESCO Joint Venture Structure

## The model

A **ZESCO co-owned Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)** — a Zambian-registered company
jointly owned by the private developer and ZESCO Limited, operating a solar plant
under a 20–25 year take-or-pay PPA.

## Live precedent: JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| SPV name | JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited |
| Private partner | Jigsaw Investments Limited |
| Government partner | ZESCO Limited |
| Project | 100 MW Chirundu Solar Plant |
| Location | Mwiinga Village, Chirundu District (285 ha) |
| Total cost | USD 90 million |
| Grid connection | 14 km, 330 kV line to Kafue Gorge Lower switching station |
| Plant life | 25 years |
| Groundbreaking | December 2025 |

> *"The project is being developed through a joint venture between Jigsaw Investments and ZESCO."*
> — ZESCO MD Justin Loongo, December 2025

## SPV ownership

```
SPV — e.g. "YourCo Solar Zambia Limited"
├── Developer / operator (private)   →  51–70% equity
├── ZESCO Limited                    →  30–49% equity
└── Community trust (optional)       →   5–10% equity
```

## What each party contributes

| Party | Contribution |
|-------|-------------|
| Developer (you) | Engineering credentials (CEng / EIZ), full operational management (25 yrs), project development, CEO / Technical Director |
| ZESCO | Capital facilitation (DFI debt), land, grid connection, government permitting, institutional DFI relationships |

## Revenue flow

```
PPA income
    └→ Debt service (~8–10 years)
        └→ O&M costs
            └→ Developer salary (CEO / Technical Director, Year 1+)
                └→ Equity dividends
                       ├── Developer (e.g. 60%): ~$312,000/yr (10 MW post-payback)
                       └── ZESCO      (e.g. 40%): ~$208,000/yr (10 MW post-payback)
```

# 09 — Developer Roadmap

## Overview

University (EEE, UK) → CEng (IET) → Africa experience → ZESCO JV partner
Total timeline: **~10–12 years** from now to first plant in commercial operation.

## Critical milestones (in order, cannot be skipped)

1. IET CEng — the master key; converts to EIZ registration via reciprocal pathway
2. HV power engineering experience (protection relays, grid connection, underground cable)
3. Africa project experience (DFI-funded energy projects)
4. EIZ overseas membership (Zambian professional status from the UK)
5. PACRA company registration in Zambia (can be done remotely)
6. In-person ZIMEC / ZESCO meeting (deals are built in rooms, not emails)
7. ZESCO JV SHA signed

## Phase 3 — Build Africa experience (years 3–6)

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Move to consultancy with Africa energy projects | AtkinsRéalis, Mott MacDonald, Aurecon, WSP |
| Get onto Africa energy projects | Grid connection (Kenya, Nigeria), HV cable, substation protection |
| Achieve CEng through IET | 4–6 years post-graduation; five competency categories |
| Apply for EIZ Member (not Associate) | CEng (IET) recognised for reciprocal EIZ registration |
| Attend ZIMEC or Africa Energy Forum | In-person networking with ZESCO staff and IPP developers |

### Why CEng is the key credential for Zambia

- Internationally recognised
- EIZ reciprocal pathway → your UK chartership = Zambian professional status
- No starting from scratch when you arrive — you are already credentialled
- ZESCO will not take you seriously as a JV partner without it
