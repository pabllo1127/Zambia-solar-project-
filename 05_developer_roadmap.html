Achieve CEng through IET
4–6 years post-graduation. Five competency categories. Professional review interview. Your HV power and Africa project experience fills this portfolio naturally. This is the master key.
the credential that opens ZESCO
Apply for EIZ Member status (not Associate)
CEng (IET) is recognised for reciprocal EIZ registration. Your UK chartership converts directly to Zambian professional status. You arrive in Zambia already credentialled.
eiz.org.zm
Attend ZIMEC or Africa Energy Forum in person
ZIMEC: annual, Kitwe. Africa Energy Forum: annual, Europe. Meet ZESCO engineers, IDC reps, and IPP developers face-to-face. The JV conversation starts in a room, not an email.
ZIMEC — Kitwe · Africa Energy Forum
yr 6
Phase 4 — position for Zambia
years 6–8 · build the deal before you move
Register Zambian company with PACRA (remote)
Can be done remotely via a Zambian agent. Cost under £500. Creates your legal development entity. The difference between a foreign engineer with an idea and a Zambian-registered developer with a proposal.
pacra.org.zm
Write your ZESCO JV proposal document
6–8 pages: your credentials, specific site (e.g. Kasama), capacity (10–20 MW), proposed equity split (open 70/30), operational plan, financial summary. Tie it to one of ZESCO's five named sites.
Visit Zambia for 2 weeks
ZIMEC in Kitwe. ZESCO Business Development meeting in Lusaka. Site visit to your proposed location. In-person presence transforms you from a name in an email to a face, a credential, and a concrete proposal.
in person changes everything
Engage Zambian legal counsel
Corpus Legal Practitioners or Musa Dudhia & Co (Lusaka). Key document: Shareholder Agreement (SHA). Protect: equity %, operational control, anti-dilution clause, management fee rights.
yr 8
Phase 5 — execute
years 8–10 · SHA signed, plant builds, you relocate
Sign ZESCO JV Shareholder Agreement
You: 60–70% equity, CEO and Technical Director. ZESCO: 30–40%. Both parties hold equity permanently for 25-year plant life.
the document that changes your financial future
Register SPV, obtain ERB generation licence, close DFI financing
SPV registered with PACRA. ERB generation licence = legal right to generate and sell electricity. ZESCO's DFI relationships unlock AfDB/IFC concessional debt (5–9%) — repaid from PPA revenue over 8–10 years.
Build and commission — you are Technical Director
Appoint EPC contractor. Supervise all electrical works. ZESCO interconnection. Protection relay commissioning. COD triggers PPA revenue. You draw salary from Day 1.
Post-payback: you live off the equity
Year 8–10: debt fully repaid. Net to equity ~$521K/yr (10 MW at $0.05/kWh). Your 60%: ~$312K/yr for 12–15 years. Use track record to negotiate Plant 2 with ZESCO on better terms.
~$312K/yr post-payback at 60% equity (10 MW)
