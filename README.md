# Using Solar Energy for Decentralised Power Generation in a Cost-Effective Manner and Habitat Restoration

> Research, feasibility, and development repository for decentralised solar PV power generation
> framework targeting industrial applications in Zambia — structured as a ZESCO joint venture
> (PPP / Special Purpose Vehicle model).

---

## Project summary

Zambia faces a structural energy crisis: over 80% of installed generation capacity is
hydroelectric, rendered acutely vulnerable by the 2023–2024 drought that cut Kariba Dam
output to 215 MW against a nameplate of 1,080 MW. The mining sector — which accounts for
50–60% of national power demand — was asked to curtail consumption by 40%. GDP growth was
revised down from 2.3% to 1.2%. The economic cost is estimated at USD 1.3 billion (~5% of GDP).

This project develops a framework for distributed solar PV generation that:
- Uses **small-to-medium plants (1–50 MW)** targeting industrial off-takers
- Transmits power via **underground XLPE cable** to eliminate habitat disruption
- Integrates into **existing ZESCO substations** to avoid new grid infrastructure costs
- Co-locates an **agri-ecological system** (goat grazing, apiculture, irrigation) for secondary revenue
- Is structured as a **ZESCO joint venture SPV** — developer holds majority equity, ZESCO holds a stake, 20–25 year take-or-pay PPA

## The ZESCO JV model

The target structure is a co-owned SPV — established precedent from the
**JIGSCO Energy Corporation Limited** (Jigsaw Investments + ZESCO, 100 MW Chirundu
Solar Plant, USD 90M, groundbreaking December 2025).

```
SPV — e.g. "YourCo Solar Zambia Limited"
├── Developer / operator (you)   →  60–70% equity
├── ZESCO Limited                →  30–40% equity
└── Community trust (optional)   →   5–10% equity

Revenue:  PPA income → debt service → O&M → salary → equity dividends
Timeline: Build (12–18 months) → payback (8–10 years) → pure profit (15+ years)
```

ZESCO sites actively seeking JV partners: Siavonga · Chipata · Mumbwa · Kasama · Kafue Gorge Lower
